\section{Infer events}

  - MO trigger: Market order triggers new limit orders (momentum following)
  - MO impact: Market order causes cancellations (adverse selection)
  - queue mgmt: Simultaneous limit order + cancellation (queue management)
  - spread change: Cancellation affects bid-ask spread
  - liquidity: Limit order affects inside spread (liquidity provision)
  - price impact: Market order moves prices immediately
  - cross trade: Trading activity on both bid and ask sides
  - cross liquidity: Liquidity provision on both sides
  - cross cancel: Liquidity removal from both sides
  - cascade: Chain reaction of market impact
  - impact: Full impact chain (trade → liquidity → price)
  - rebalance: Complex liquidity rebalancing
  - complex: Large simultaneous event bursts

\section{suspecious}
use order type and levels to infer pump and dump, spoofing, and wash trading

(When detecting spoofing in LOB:
Large order imbalance suddenly appears far from normal depth.
Orders are canceled quickly (milliseconds/seconds) once price moves.
Spoof orders often never trade (low fill ratio).
Appears in clusters around best bid/ask ± few ticks.)
